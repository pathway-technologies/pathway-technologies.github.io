## The Problem

Standard DevOps practices are optimised for speed and flexibility, but do not
inherently support the traceability and auditability required by regulated
engineering environments.

- Lack of clear traceability between requirements, code, and artefacts
- Manual or fragmented compliance processes
- Build pipelines that cannot be easily audited or verified

---

## Approach

DevOps pipelines are designed with explicit attention to reproducibility,
traceability, and alignment with engineering standards.

### 1. Assess

Review of current tooling, workflows, and compliance constraints.

### 2. Design

Definition of pipeline architecture, artefact flow, and traceability model.

### 3. Implement

Deployment and integration of pipelines, tooling, and supporting processes.

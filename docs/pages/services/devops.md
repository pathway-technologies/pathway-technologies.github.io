<section class="w3-container w3-padding-32 w3-light-grey">
  <h2>The Problem</h2>

  <p>
    Standard DevOps practices are often optimised for speed and flexibility,
    but do not inherently support the traceability and auditability required
    by regulated engineering environments.
  </p>

  <ul>
    <li>Lack of clear traceability between requirements, code, and artefacts</li>
    <li>Manual or fragmented compliance processes</li>
    <li>Build pipelines that cannot be easily audited or verified</li>
  </ul>
</section>

<section class="w3-container w3-padding-32">
  <h2>Approach</h2>

  <p>
    DevOps pipelines are designed and implemented with explicit attention
    to traceability, reproducibility, and alignment with industry standards.
  </p>

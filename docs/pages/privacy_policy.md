This page explains what personal data may be processed when you visit this website,
how it is used, and your rights in relation to that data.

## Data Collection and Use

### Website Access

When you access this website, basic technical information may be recorded by the hosting infrastructure.

This may include:

- IP address  
- Browser type and version  
- Date and time of access  

This information is used for the operation, security, and maintenance of the website.

### Email Communication

If you contact Pathway Technologies by email, your email address and any information
you provide will be processed for the purpose of responding to your enquiry.

This information is not used for marketing purposes without your consent.

### Newsletter

If you subscribe to the Pathway Technologies newsletter or mailing list, personal data (such as your name and email address) will be collected and processed for the purpose of providing relevant updates and information.

Subscription is based on your explicit consent. You may withdraw your consent at any time by using the unsubscribe link included in each communication or by contacting us directly.

Newsletter distribution may be handled by a third-party email service provider,
selected with consideration for data protection and regulatory alignment.

### Embedded Content

This website may include embedded content from third-party platforms, such as Vimeo or YouTube.

Embedded content may collect data independently of this website.
Once you interact with such content, the third-party platform
acts as a separate data controller.

### Analytics (Future Use)

This website may in future use analytics tools to understand how it is used.

If such tools are introduced:

- only necessary data will be collected  
- appropriate consent mechanisms will be implemented where required  
- this policy will be updated accordingly  

## Data Security

Appropriate technical and organisational measures are applied to protect
personal data against unauthorised access, disclosure, or loss.

## Business Changes

In the event of a business reorganisation (such as a merger or acquisition),
personal data may be transferred as part of that process, subject to applicable law.

## Cookies

At present, this website does not use cookies for analytics or tracking purposes.

However, embedded third-party content (such as Vimeo or YouTube)
may set cookies when interacted with.

If additional cookies are introduced in the future, a separate Cookie Policy and
consent mechanism will be provided where required.

## Data Retention

Personal data is retained only for as long as necessary to fulfil its purpose,
including responding to enquiries and maintaining the operation and security of the website.

## Data Sharing

Personal data is not sold or shared with third parties, except where necessary
to operate the website (e.g. hosting and infrastructure providers) or where required by law.

## Data Controller and Jurisdiction

This website is operated by Pathway Technologies Ltd., a company registered in Hong Kong.

Pathway Technologies Ltd. acts as the data controller for any personal data processed in connection with this website.

Contact: contact@pathway-technologies.com

## Legal Basis for Processing

Where personal data is processed, the following legal bases may apply:

- Consent: where you have explicitly agreed to the processing (e.g. for a newsletter)  
- Contractual necessity: where processing is required to respond to or fulfil a request  
- Legal obligations: where data must be processed to comply with legal requirements  
- Legitimate interests: where processing is necessary for the operation and security of the website, provided such interests are not overridden by your rights and interests  

## International Data Transfers

Due to the distributed nature of the Internet infrastructure used to operate this website, personal data may be processed in multiple jurisdictions.

In particular:

- Website hosting is provided by GitHub (a US-based service)  
- Supporting infrastructure may be located within the European Union  
- Email communication may be processed by third-party email providers  

Where personal data is processed outside the European Economic Area (EEA), appropriate safeguards are applied as required by applicable data protection laws.

Given the limited nature of data collected by this website, any such transfers are minimal and incidental to the operation of the service.

## Regulatory Approach

Pathway Technologies Ltd. is not established within the European Union.
However, services are frequently provided to organisations operating under
European data protection requirements.

Accordingly, data handling practices are designed to align, as far as
practicable, with the principles of the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR),
including:

- Data minimisation  
- Transparency and clarity of purpose  
- Appropriate safeguards for data processing and transfer  

This approach is adopted to support the expectations of clients operating in
regulated environments, and to provide a consistent and predictable standard of
data protection across jurisdictions.

This approach also reflects a broader trend toward alignment with
GDPR-style data protection frameworks across multiple jurisdictions.

## Your Rights

Under applicable data protection law (including the GDPR where relevant), you have the right to:

- Request access to your personal data  
- Request correction of inaccurate data  
- Request deletion of your personal data  
- Object to or restrict processing in certain circumstances  
- Lodge a complaint with a relevant data protection authority
